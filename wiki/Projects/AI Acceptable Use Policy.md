# AI Acceptable Use Policy

Draft and ship an AI acceptable use policy for Coastal Ridge Insurance, triggered by
the shadow-AI inventory findings. Pen: [[Jess Park]]. Chaired by [[Dana Whitfield]].

## Why now

[[Sam Rivera]]'s inventory found **17 unapproved AI tools** in active use — including
free-tier chatbots where staff pasted claims notes containing customer data
([[Shadow AI]]). Customer data left the environment through tools nobody approved.

## Approach (decided 2026-06-16)

**Traffic-light tiering:**

| Tier | Meaning |
|------|---------|
| 🟢 Green | Approved for general use |
| 🟡 Yellow | Approved with conditions (data types, use cases) |
| 🔴 Red | Banned |

Tier assignments live in the AI tool register ([[Sam Rivera]], building).
