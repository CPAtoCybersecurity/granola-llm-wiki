# Vendor Risk Management

Ongoing responsibility: every vendor that touches Coastal Ridge data gets a security
review before signature, and material findings get an explicit decision (remediate,
accept, or walk). No finish line — this is a standard, not a project.

## The working pattern (as practiced)

1. Procurement catches the vendor at intake ([[Taylor Brooks]]) and pairs with a
   security assessor ([[Jess Park]]).
2. SOC 2 Type II walkthrough — exceptions read closely, not counted
   ([[SOC 2 Reports]]).
3. Findings become **conditions of signature** (e.g. SSO tier requirement), a
   **risk acceptance** with an owner, or a walk-away.
4. Identity lifecycle questions (SSO, SCIM offboarding) checked by IT
   ([[Omar Haddad]]) — vendor-side stale accounts are a recurring leak path.
