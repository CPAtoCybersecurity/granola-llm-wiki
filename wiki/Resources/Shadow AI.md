# Shadow AI

Unapproved AI tools adopted by staff without security review — the AI-era version of
shadow IT. Reference page; the policy response lives at [[AI Acceptable Use Policy]].

## What the inventory found (2026-06-16)

[[Sam Rivera]]'s two-week inventory at Coastal Ridge: **17 unapproved AI tools** in
active use. Highest-risk finding: free-tier chatbots where staff pasted **claims notes
containing customer data**. Free tiers typically reserve the right to train on inputs —
so customer data didn't just leave the environment, it may have left permanently.

## Why it happens

Staff aren't malicious — the tools are genuinely useful and the approved path either
doesn't exist or is slower. That's why the response is a tiered allow-list
(traffic-light model) rather than a ban: give the demand a safe outlet.
